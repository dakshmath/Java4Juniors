Our Mission

Java4Juniors was founded in 2024 to address the gap in computer science education for students in Howard County. We believe every student should have the opportunity to learn real programming skills, regardless of their background.

Our unique peer-to-peer learning model connects students with experienced high school mentors who teach Java programming fundamentals in an engaging, accessible way.
